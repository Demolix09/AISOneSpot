Letter from the Director

April 2026

  • To our AIS Families,
  • Our deepest appreciation goes to the PTO and faculty for the fantastic Family Day held on March 14th.
  • We celebrate the incredible efforts and achievements of our students across these fields:
  • AASCA MUN: Hosting nearly 350 regional participants, AIS shined as a premier venue for diplomatic debate. Volleyball: Our team fought hard to secure a commendable 6th place overall. Chess: Our new Elementary squad excelled with every member hitting the top 20 and scoring a 6th place finish. Mathematics: Exceptional results were highlights, particularly the Silver Medal earned by Honggeun Kim. Basketball: The excitement continues as we host the ACTION League Final on April 16th at 2:00 pm.
  • The dedication of our athletes remains inspiring, and we wish the very best to our teams heading into the upcoming Swimming, Tennis, Volleyball, and Soccer competitions this month.
  • Go Falcons!
  • Warmly with holiday wishes, Director Kattya Alvarado.
